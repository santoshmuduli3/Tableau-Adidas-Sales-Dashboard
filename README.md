# Tableau-Adidas-Sales-Dashboard

**Key Business Insights**
1. Strong Financial Performance
      Nearly $900M in sales with an operating margin of around 37%.
2. Men's Street Footwear is the Star Product
      Highest operating margin (44.61%).
      Highest units sold (593K).
3. West Region Leads the Business
      Highest sales and highest operating profit.
4. West Gear is the Best Retail Partner
      Generates over $242M in sales.
5. Physical Stores Still Dominate
      In-store sales outperform online and outlet channels.
6. Strong Growth During 2021
      Sales increased significantly compared with 2020.
7. New York is the Highest Revenue State
      Followed by California and Florida.

**Business Recommendations**
1. Increase inventory and marketing for Men's Street Footwear, as it excels in both sales volume and profitability.
2. Strengthen partnerships with West Gear, Foot Locker, and Sports Direct, which contribute the highest sales.
3. Invest in expanding the Online sales channel through digital marketing and improved customer experience.
4. Focus growth initiatives in the Midwest region to reduce regional performance gaps.
5. Replicate successful strategies from New York, California, and Florida in lower-performing states.
6. Continue expanding in the West region while identifying opportunities in underperforming regions.
